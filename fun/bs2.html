MORE INFO

As you might know, Beat Games no longer owns the rights to the Beat Saber VR game or Beat Saber related products. As Meta now COMPLETELY owns the Beat Saber IP.

WHAT DOES THIS MEAN?

It means you get more of the game that you love! We understand that changes can be scary, but Meta has agreed to listen to every word spoken by the Beat Saber community. Below is a list of the spectacular new changes that are coming to Beat Saber!
  • The game is now exclusive to Meta products.
  • A whole new game!
  • An even bigger community!
  • Even more updates!
  • New graphics
  • Completely new features which can be seen listed below!

NEW FEATURES

Easy Anti Cheat

Implementing EAC should've been something that had been done years ago, and we sincerely apologize. As other popular VR games have been implementing EAC into their games, it about time we do as well. Goodbye cheaters!

Beat Sage

We know you Meta Quest users have been wanting custom songs for quite a long while... so here it is! With this new feature, make any map you want, with the click of a button! This feature will completely replace normal custom songs, as they are now obsolete.

What is Beat Sage?

Beat Sage is an AI custom song generator capable of taking an audio file or YouTube link and converting it into a fast-paced, energetic map. Quest users will be able to sync their songs between their headset and the mobile app via a new page in the devices tab. Simply paste the link to a music video or upload an mp3 to the app and it will automatically generate the map and sync it to Beat Saber the next time you open the game.

Beat-Pass and BeatCoin

It's hard to keep a game up without monetization. We have added these optionally purchasable items into the game. We hope you enjoy the new content!
Beat-Pass! (Only accessible in multiplayer.)
Beatcoin [ˈbɪ:tˌkɔɪ̯n]. Use this item to buy other items or songs in the shop! Pricing starts at $10.00 per coin.
